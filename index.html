About me

My background is in Optimization, Machine Learning, Signal Processing, and Wireless Networks.

My current research focuses on the theory and applications of Optimization and Machine Learning to large-scale networked systems such as Internet-of-Things.

My Ph.D advisor is Prof. Georgios B. Giannakis.
