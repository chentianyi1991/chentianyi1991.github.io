Bio

Open positions: I am looking for motivated PhD students and postdocs at Cornell University with strong backgrounds in optimization, ML systems, or AI hardware. Please email a CV to apply.

I am an Associate Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Cornell Tech and Cornell University (since Fall 2025), and a member of the FIND group. I am on leave from RPI, where I was promoted to Associate Professor with tenure in 2025. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Minnesota and my B.Sc. from Fudan University with the highest honors. Selected honors: IEEE SPS Best PhD Dissertation Award (2020, inaugural), NSF CAREER (2021), Amazon Research Award (2022), and IEEE SPS Young Author Best Paper Award (2024).

Research overview. My group works at the interface of large language models and the AI hardware and systems that run them — developing principled optimization that aligns models to competing objectives and trains them within the energy budget of physical hardware.
