About me

Welcome to my homepage!

I'm a second-year Ph.D. in the IEOR Department at UC Berkeley. My advisor is Prof. Xin Guo.

Prior to Berkeley, I received my M.S. in Pure Mathematics and Statistics from University of Cambridge, and B.S. in Mathematics from Nanjing University. Over the years, I have also been very fortunate to work and collaborate with many amazing persons on particular projects.
