About me

I'm working with the Signal Processing in Networking and Communications (SPiNCOM) group at University of Minnesota.

My background is in optimization, statistical signal processing, machine learning, and wireless networks.

My current research focuses on building fundamental connections among methodologies from the optimization, learning and networking communities,
and developing inter-disciplinary approaches for Internet-of-Things (IoT).

My advisor is Prof. Georgios B. Giannakis.
